Franklin Breaks Record

Cities across the Clark County area set new records Friday for daytime temperatures this time of year as unseasonably warm and dry conditions continue to grip the state.

Eleven locations eclipsed heat records for May 9th, including Greenville where a high of 78 degrees beat a record of 75 degrees set in 2006, the National Weather Service reported. Other Clark County towns to break previous heat records included Springfield and Oberlin.
