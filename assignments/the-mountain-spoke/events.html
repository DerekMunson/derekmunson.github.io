Upcoming Events

August 17 & 18, 2018

The annual Cycle the Continental Divide bike race is one of the most recognized and important off-pavement cycling races in the world. The race route crisscrosses the Continental Divide from north to south starting in Banff, Alberta, Canada and finishing at the US/Mexico border in Antelope Wells, New Mexico.

Event Details

Cost: $375 per rider
Starting Location: Banff National Park (Alberta, CAN)
Ending Location: Antelope Wells, New Mexico (USA)
Total Mileage: 2,774 miles
Ride Duration: ~16 days total (depends on your pace)

Route Map

Trace the path you'll take from cold and snowy Banff to hot and sunny New Mexico!
