Adventure Tours

Here at The Mountain Spoke, we offer tours and adventures for riders of all ages and skill levels. Our most popular rides are our three daily offerings: the Hill Climber, the Scenic Route, and the Road Warrior.

The Hill Climber

This one is not for the faint of heart! Over the course of this 15 mile ride, you'll ascend 6,580 feet into the wide open Montana sky, traveling up through Beacon Point on your way to Wilson Peak, where you'll have the opportunity to enjoy some of the best views Central Montana has to offer.

The Scenic Route

Enjoy a scenic ride around Wheeler Mountain! This low-impact ride is great for riders of all skill levels and ages. Following well-groomed forest roads, you'll gently ascend 400 feet over the course of the 8 mile ride, which is taken at a leisurely pace, allowing you to drink in the natural beauty all around you.

The Road Warrior

Push that road bike to its limits. This ride takes place around the city of Bozeman, but just because we're not climbing hills doesn't mean it's not a challenge!
